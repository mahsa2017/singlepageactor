AMIR AGHAEE

Amir Aghaee was born in Urmia, Azerbaijan, Iran and has been acting professionally since 2001. He left his hometown and moved to Tehran to furthis his career in acting at the early age of fifteen. Upon receiving his diploma in Cinema from “Tehran’s School Of Cinema” in 2010, Acting as a profession has always been Amir’s calling and has been his focus and passion since early childhood. He has worked and traveled to seven different cities and has fearlessly relocated to several countries, in order to gain experience, knowledge and insights about different acting and production methods; to learn the fundamentals of stage acting and films; and to discover his joyful place in the rich and diverse acting universe.
